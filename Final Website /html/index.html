Services

What I can do for you

Music Production

I work mainly on Logic Pro X. I have experience with sound design, song arrangment, and I play piano and guitar. Let's make music together.

Record Engineering

Because I have a small home studio, I mainly focus on recording vocals, small percussion and guitar. If you have a song in mind but don't know how to record, I'm your guy.

Mix Engineering

Most of my mixing is done at my home studio using software effects and plugins. I focus on mixing vocals and instruments from strach but I also like plugins when they add color to the mix. I can mix most genres so shoot me an email and let's work.
